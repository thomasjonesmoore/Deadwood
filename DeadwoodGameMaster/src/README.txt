User Interface Elements:
The user's dice on the map reflects their current rank, this can also be seen on the stat panel on the left. 
The dice on the stat panel display that player's last roll result (rehearse counters are not automatically added to this die).
To move to a room, click on the scene card, with the trailer and casting office click on their names.
To upgrade, just click on the desired amount you wish to spend.
The panel in the bottom left displays the current player's turn.

Launching the game:
In the terminal write java Deadwood x where x is the desired amount of players
